Privacy Policy for MERN Image Search

Last Updated: October 30, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use our MERN Image Search application, we collect:

  • Basic profile information from your OAuth provider (Google, GitHub, Facebook)
  • Email address (if provided by OAuth provider)
  • Profile picture (if provided by OAuth provider)
  • Search history (search queries you perform)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate your account
  • Provide personalized search history
  • Display your profile information
  • Improve our service

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our MongoDB database. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth: For authentication
  • GitHub OAuth: For authentication
  • Facebook OAuth: For authentication
  • Unsplash API: For image search functionality
  • MongoDB Atlas: For data storage

5. Cookies and Sessions

We use session cookies to maintain your logged-in state. These cookies are essential for the application to function properly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of data collection by not using the service

7. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by logging out and contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes.
